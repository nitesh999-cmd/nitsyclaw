# NitsyClaw Pilot Decision Gate

Run this decision after:

- 10 warm contacts have been offered the pilot, or
- 14 days have passed, whichever comes first.

## Build nothing more until this is answered

Did at least 2 people pay for the pilot?

## Verdict rules

### BUILD NEXT

Use only if:

- 2+ people paid, and
- at least 1 person says they would continue, and
- WhatsApp was not the only reason they bought.

Next build:

- Customer-safe demo flow.
- Usage/cost guard.
- Tenant isolation plan only if moving beyond concierge.

### TEST AGAIN

Use if:

- 1 person paid, or
- 3+ people showed strong interest but did not pay, or
- people wanted the result but objected to the offer wording.

Next test:

- Adjust price or wedge.
- Try one narrower offer: "bill and receipt digest" only.

### PARK COMMERCIAL

Use if:

- 0 people paid from 10 warm contacts.
- People only said it was cool.
- People wanted full bank/email/calendar automation before paying.

Next move:

- Keep NitsyClaw as a personal tool.
- Stop public-sale work.
- Revisit only after a stronger painful niche appears.

## Scoring table

| Signal | Green | Yellow | Red |
|---|---|---|---|
| Paid pilots | 2+ | 1 | 0 |
| Real admin sent | 3+ people | 1-2 people | 0 people |
| Would continue | 1+ yes | maybe | no |
| Channel dependence | accepts non-WhatsApp fallback | unsure | only wants WhatsApp |
| Main pain clarity | bills/receipts/reminders clear | mixed | vague/cool idea |

## Decision sentence

Use this exact sentence after the test:

```text
NitsyClaw should [BUILD NEXT / TEST AGAIN / PARK COMMERCIAL] because [paid evidence], [usage evidence], and [main blocker].
```
