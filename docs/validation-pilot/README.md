# NitsyClaw Paid Pilot Validation Kit

Purpose: validate whether people will pay for the narrow NitsyClaw wedge before more build work.

Verdict basis: Claude Cowork recommended `TEST FIRST`, not more engineering. This kit turns that verdict into a small, measurable 14-day pilot.

## The wedge

**WhatsApp life-admin concierge for bills, receipts, expenses, due-date reminders, and weekly digest.**

Do not sell:

- Full autonomous PA.
- Live Gmail/Outlook actions.
- Bank feeds.
- Phone/SMS sending.
- Google Drive/Photos browsing.
- Public multi-user app access.

## Success criteria

Run this test with 10 warm contacts.

Proceed only if:

- At least 2 people pay AUD 39 upfront, or
- At least 3 people explicitly say yes and send real bills/receipts/admin for handling, with payment agreed.

Stop or park commercialisation if:

- 0 paid pilots from 10 warm offers.
- People say "cool idea" but do not pay.
- People only want it after Gmail/bank/phone integrations are live.

## What the customer gets

For 2 weeks:

- They send bills, receipts, admin reminders, and things they do not want to forget.
- You log reminders and expense notes.
- You summarise bills and due dates.
- You draft replies or complaint messages when needed.
- You send one weekly admin digest.

Manual delivery is allowed. The point is to validate payment and pain, not automation.

## Guardrails

- No bank login.
- No mailbox login.
- No payments made on behalf of user.
- No calls or SMS sent on behalf of user.
- No sensitive IDs requested.
- Draft-only for external messages.
- Refund if the pilot does not save time.
